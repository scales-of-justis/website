About Scales of Justis

Scales of Justis is a true freestyle rapper who specializes in off-the-top performances and battles. Born and raised in Auburn, WA, he discovered his passion for hip-hop at a young age and began writing his own rhymes. As he honed his craft, he became known for his quick wit and ability to improvise on the spot.

Today, Scales of Justis is a well-respected figure in the rap community and has competed in several local and national rap battles and competitions. His dedication to the art of freestyle has earned him a loyal fan base, and he continues to inspire and challenge other aspiring rappers to push the limits of their own creativity.
